Aitor Aguirre's website

Professor and Researcher · Mondragon University, Faculty of Engineering · Data Analysis and Cybersecurity Research Group

Aitor Aguirre-Ortuzar is a Doctor in Computer Engineering and a teaching researcher at Mondragon Unibertsitatea. He is a member of the Intelligent Systems for Industrial Systems Group, specialising in Data Analysis and Cybersecurity. He obtained his degree in Computer Engineering at Mondragon Unibertsitatea and defended his thesis in Artificial Intelligence at TECNUN in 2013.

His main line of research focuses on Trustworthy AI, addressing topics such as Scientific Machine Learning (SciML), probabilistic AI, and Explainable AI (XAI). He has worked on national and European projects, applying his expertise in industrial projects and in the Fintech sector.

He combines his research work with teaching, being a professor in the Master’s programmes in Data Analysis, Cybersecurity, and Smart Energy Systems. He is also responsible for BDVA and ETAMI activities on behalf of the university, and coordinates the AI Chair of Ulma Handling Systems.
