**Aitor Aguirre-Ortuzar** is a Doctor in Computer Engineering and Professor at Mondragon Unibertsitatea (Faculty of Engineering). He is a member of the Intelligent Systems for Industrial Systems (SISI) research group, specialising in Data Analysis and Cybersecurity.

His main research line focuses on Trustworthy AI, addressing Scientific Machine Learning (SciML), probabilistic AI, and Explainable AI (XAI). He has participated in national and European research projects and coordinates the Ulma Handling Systems – MGEP AI Chair.
