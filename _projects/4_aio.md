**AIO** (Adimenezko Industria Optimizazioa) is a project funded by the Diputación Foral de Gipuzkoa (2022–2023) focused on applying AI techniques to industrial optimisation problems. The project developed intelligent methods for improving efficiency in manufacturing and industrial processes.

**Funding:** Diputación Foral de Gipuzkoa (DFG)  
**Duration:** 2022 – 2023  
**Role:** Principal Investigator
