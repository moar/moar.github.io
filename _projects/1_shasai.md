**SHASAI** (Secure Hardware and Software for AI systems) is a European-funded project running from November 2025 to April 2029. The project addresses security challenges in AI systems at both the hardware and software levels, combining cybersecurity research with trustworthy AI methodologies.

**Funding:** European Union  
**Duration:** 2025 – 2029  
**Role:** Researcher  
**PI:** Aitor Urbieta
