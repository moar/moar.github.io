**SMARTENCITY** (Towards Smart Zero CO2 Cities across Europe) is a European project (2016–2022) focused on developing and deploying smart city technologies to achieve near-zero CO₂ emissions in urban districts. The project combined energy efficiency, data analytics and IoT solutions across multiple European cities.

**Funding:** European Union  
**Duration:** 2016 – 2022  
**Role:** Researcher  
**PI:** Félix Larrinaga Barrenechea
