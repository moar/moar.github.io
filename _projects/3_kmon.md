**KMON** (Kuantika MONDRAGON) is an MCC-funded project (2023–2024) exploring the transition from classical computing to Quantum Machine Learning. The project investigates how quantum computing algorithms can enhance AI performance for industrial applications.

**Funding:** MCC (Mondragon Cooperative Corporation)  
**Duration:** 2023 – 2024  
**Role:** Principal Investigator
