**FEDIA** (Federated Learning Vertical para IA) is a Basque Government-funded project (2024–2026) focused on vertical federated learning applied to AI systems. The project explores privacy-preserving machine learning techniques that allow organisations to collaboratively train AI models without sharing raw data.

**Funding:** Basque Government (GV)  
**Duration:** 2024 – 2026  
**Role:** Researcher  
**PI:** Álvaro Pérez
