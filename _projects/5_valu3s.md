**VALU3S** is a European H2020 project (2020–2023) dedicated to the verification and validation of safety and security properties in automated systems. The project developed novel V&V methodologies and tools applicable to autonomous and cyber-physical systems across multiple industrial domains.

**Funding:** European Union (H2020)  
**Duration:** 2020 – 2023  
**Role:** Researcher  
**PI:** Behrooz Sangchoolie
