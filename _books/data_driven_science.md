A comprehensive treatment of data-driven methods in science and engineering, covering dimensionality reduction, sparse sensing, machine learning and dynamical systems. The book bridges classical mathematical methods with modern machine learning, with a strong emphasis on Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) and Physics-Informed approaches.
